Who We Are - What We Believe In

Aether United is a revolutionary kind of esports company.
We use blockchain to fund ourself and futarchy to let esports fans participate in strategical decisions about the gaming team.

We believe in the wisdom of the crowd, and we believe that a stronger connection between teams and their fan base is a win-win situation.

We are the only crowdpowered (i.e. fan-powered) esports organization.
We exist to make our teams great; to make esports great!
We are the People's Rebublic of Esports!
