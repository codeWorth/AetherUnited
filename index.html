Intro to Aether United

Aether United is a revolutionary esports brand.
By using blockchain technology, we will have unprecedented financial transparency. Additionally, our use of prediction markets allows use to harness the wisdom of the crowd to make more informed decisions.

We believe in the wisdom of the crowd, and we believe that a stronger connection between teams and their fan base is a win-win situation.

As the only crowdpowered esports organization, our main goal is to make our teams great and improve the esports ecosystem.
